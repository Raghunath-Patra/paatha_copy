Exothermic vs Endothermic Reactions

🌡️ Energy Changes in Chemical Reactions

Understand how energy is involved in chemical reactions!

🔥
Exothermic Reactions
Reactants → Products + Heat

Heat is released to the surroundings

Examples: Burning, Respiration, Rusting
❄️
Endothermic Reactions
Reactants + Heat → Products

Heat is absorbed from the surroundings

Examples: Photosynthesis, Decomposition

Types of Decomposition Reactions

⚡ Decomposition by Different Energy Sources

Learn how different forms of energy can cause decomposition!

🔥
Thermal Decomposition
Heat energy breaks bonds

Example: CaCO₃ →[Heat]→ CaO + CO₂

⚡
Electrolytic Decomposition
Electric energy breaks bonds

Example: H₂O →[Electricity]→ H₂ + O₂

☀️
Photolytic Decomposition
Light energy breaks bonds

Example: AgCl →[Sunlight]→ Ag + Cl₂
