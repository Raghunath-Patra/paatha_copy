Corrosion
Metal + O₂ + H₂O → Metal Oxide

Metals react with environment to form unwanted compounds

Examples: Iron rusting, silver tarnishing, copper patina
🥜
Rancidity
Fats/Oils + O₂ → Rancid Products

Oxidation of fats and oils causing bad smell and taste

Examples: Stale chips, old cooking oil, spoiled nuts

Food Preservation Simulator

🥫 Explore Food Preservation Methods

Discover how different preservation techniques prevent rancidity!

🛡️ Antioxidants

Substances that prevent oxidation of fats and oils

Vitamin C, Vitamin E, BHT, BHA

🔒 Airtight Containers

Prevent oxygen contact with food

Vacuum packing, sealed jars

💨 Nitrogen Flushing

Replace oxygen with inert nitrogen gas

Chip bags, coffee packaging

❄️ Refrigeration

Low temperature slows oxidation reactions

Fridges, freezers
