What Are Irrational Numbers?

📚 Definition: A number 's' is called irrational if it cannot be written in the form p/q, where p and q are integers and q ≠ 0.

🎯 Key Insight: Irrational numbers have infinite, non-repeating decimal expansions. They "fill the gaps" between rational numbers on the number line!
