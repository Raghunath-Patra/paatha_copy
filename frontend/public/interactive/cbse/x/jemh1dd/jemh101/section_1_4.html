🧮 Formulas for Three Numbers

🎯 Important Discovery: For three numbers p, q, r: HCF(p, q, r) × LCM(p, q, r) ≠ p × q × r

But we have special formulas that connect them! These advanced formulas are used in competitive mathematics and computer science.

📐 Advanced Formulas

LCM(p, q, r) = [p × q × r × HCF(p, q, r)] / [HCF(p, q) × HCF(q, r) × HCF(p, r)]
This formula connects the LCM of three numbers to their individual HCFs and overall HCF

Key Takeaways

🎓 What You've Learned:
  • Prime numbers are the building blocks of all natural numbers
  • Every composite number has a unique prime factorization
  • √2, √3, √5 are irrational and can't be written as fractions
  • HCF and LCM are related by the formula HCF × LCM = a × b (for two numbers)
  • Proof by contradiction is a powerful mathematical technique
  • Number theory has practical applications in cryptography and computing
