1. Atomic Mass

🔬 Atomic Mass Standard

Based on Carbon-12 (¹²C) = exactly 12 amu

1 amu = 1.66056 × 10⁻²⁴ g

Definition: Atomic mass is the mass of an atom expressed in atomic mass units (amu), where 1 amu equals 1/12th the mass of a carbon-12 atom.

3. Molecular Mass

🧪 Molecular Mass

Sum of atomic masses of all atoms in a molecule

Definition: Molecular mass is obtained by multiplying the atomic mass of each element by the number of its atoms and adding them together.
