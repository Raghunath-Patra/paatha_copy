🎯 Why Water is Essential:

  • 90% of plant: Water makes up most of plant structure
  • Nutrient transport: Carries minerals from soil to all parts
  • Photosynthesis: Required for making food from sunlight
  • Temperature control: Protects from frost and heat
  • Germination: Seeds need moisture to sprout
