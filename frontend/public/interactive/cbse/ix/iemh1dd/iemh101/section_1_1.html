Key Concepts Summary

🔑 Important Points to Remember:

  • Natural Numbers (ℕ): Counting numbers {1, 2, 3, 4, ...}
  • Whole Numbers (W): Natural numbers + 0 {0, 1, 2, 3, ...}
  • Integers (ℤ): All positive and negative whole numbers {..., -2, -1, 0, 1, 2, ...}
  • Rational Numbers (ℚ): Numbers that can be written as p/q where p, q are integers and q ≠ 0
  • Every number system contains all previous ones: ℕ ⊂ W ⊂ ℤ ⊂ ℚ
  • Between any two rational numbers, there are infinitely many rational numbers
