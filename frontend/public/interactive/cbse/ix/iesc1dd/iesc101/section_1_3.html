üåü The Three States of Matter

üéØ Key Insight

Matter around us exists in three different states due to variations in particle characteristics!

üßä

SOLID STATE

Properties:

  • Fixed shape & volume
  • Particles tightly packed
  • Strong forces of attraction
  • Negligible compressibility
  • Rigid structure
üíß

LIQUID STATE

Properties:

  • Fixed volume, no fixed shape
  • Particles loosely packed
  • Moderate forces of attraction
  • Can flow (fluid)
  • Takes shape of container
üí®

GAS STATE

Properties:

  • No fixed shape or volume
  • Particles far apart
  • Weak forces of attraction
  • Highly compressible
  • Fills entire container
